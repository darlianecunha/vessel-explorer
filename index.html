Vessel Efficiency Predictor

Predict a ship's operational CO2 efficiency grade (A-E) from EU MRV attributes. The grade-defining CO2 is never used as input.

Vessel attributes

Model: multinomial logistic regression trained on 1,570 EU MRV vessels. Runs entirely in your browser.
